# BI For Operational Efficiency in Hospitals

## Business Understanding
This project focuses on the healthcare industry, specifically a town hospital facing operational inefficiencies such as long patient waiting times, uneven workload distribution among doctors and departments, and declining patient satisfaction.
The objective of this Business Intelligence solution is to provide hospital management with actionable insights to optimize staff scheduling, balance departmental workload, reduce waiting times, and improve patient experience.

## BI Objectives
- Analyze patient waiting times across departments and shifts
- Evaluate doctor workload and utilization
- Identify overutilized and underutilized departments and doctors
- Assess the relationship between waiting time and patient satisfaction
